Categories How was it made?
White Tea Real white tea is made from the handpicked buds of the Camellia Sinensis plant
Green Tea The leaf is picked, withered, heated and then rolled before drying and sifting
Oolong Tea The teas are picked, often 3 leaves and a bud, withered, fermented, then heated, rolled and dried.
Black Tea Black tea is picked, withered, to reduce moisture and make the leaf pliable, then rolled, fermented and dried before sifting.
